# Contributing to Avalonix
We value your contribution to the development of Avalonix. There are many ways to contribute to development.

The best way to help us is to make a [pull request](https://docs.github.com/en/pull-requests/collaborating-with-pull-requests/proposing-changes-to-your-work-with-pull-requests/creating-a-pull-request) 
with your fixes and adding new features.

Here's what you need for this.
- For Rust code, you need [rust-analyzer](https://github.com/rust-lang/rust-analyzer) or
[rustfmt](https://github.com/rust-lang/rustfmt) to maintain code style. Ideally, you should write tests for each data structure.
- JS and TS also require some kind of formatter. For example, [TypeScript Language Server](https://github.com/typescript-language-server/typescript-language-server).

You can also simply [report an error](https://docs.github.com/en/issues/tracking-your-work-with-issues/using-issues/creating-an-issue), help with the design, or even just recommend Avalonix to your friends.
